Our Story

Math tutors focused on student success.

Aim High began in our final year of high school with a simple idea, that a love for math is something worth sharing. Starting as informal sessions with few students quickly revealed something we had never expected: the most effective tutoring doesn't come from a teacher behind a desk; rather it comes from someone who sat in the same seat not long ago, remembering exactly where the confusion sets in. That insight shaped everything we built from there. Within nine months, we transformed a passion project into a business with 17 students as we continue delivering high-impact tutoring.
